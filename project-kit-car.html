Overview

Built a Factory 5 Mk4 Roadster from the ground up with my father over two years, completing all mechanical assembly, designing custom electrical systems, and fabricating brake lines. The project required interpreting technical manuals, precision fabrication, and systematic assembly of complex automotive systems.

Challenge

Transform a kit of fiberglass body panels and steel frame components into a fully functional, roadworthy vehicle. Required custom fabrication, electrical system design, and solving numerous fitment and compatibility issues without step-by-step instructions.

Solution

Systematically assembled all mechanical systems following technical manuals and engineering drawings. Designed and implemented custom dashboard layout with optimized switch and dial placement. Modified and routed complete wiring harness for electrical system integration. Fabricated and fitted body panels using metal shears, drills, and riveting techniques. Customized brake lines using bending, cutting, and flaring tools to ensure proper routing and hydraulic integrity.

Build Specifications

  • Engine: Blueprint Engines 427 Small Block V8
  • Body: Fiberglass construction
  • Frame: Steel chassis
  • Transmission: Manual
  • Custom Systems: Dashboard layout, complete wiring harness, brake system

Key Contributions

  • Custom dashboard design with switch and dial placement optimization
  • Complete electrical system wiring harness routing and integration
  • Body panel fabrication and fitting using Cleco fasteners and riveting
  • Brake line customization with proper flaring and routing
  • Mechanical assembly following technical specifications
  • Component compatibility verification and torque specification adherence

Tools & Techniques

  • Metal fabrication: shears, drills, grinders, saws
  • Fastening: Cleco fasteners, riveting tools, torque wrench
  • Brake work: Line bending, cutting, and flaring tools
  • Assembly: Engine hoist, hand tools, precision measuring
